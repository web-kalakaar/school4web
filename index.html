About Us

Building Minds,
Inspiring Futures

Established in 1994, Radhe Krishna Senior Secondary School School has been at the forefront of progressive education in India. We blend rigorous academics with holistic development, nurturing every child's unique potential.

Our CBSE-affiliated curriculum is enhanced with international pedagogy, smart classrooms, and an environment where curiosity thrives and leadership is born.

CBSE Affiliated ISO 9001:2015 Certified National Award Winner Digital Campus Green School Boarding Available

Highlights & Features

Everything Your Child
Deserves to Thrive

We offer world-class infrastructure and programs designed to develop the leaders of tomorrow.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive digital boards, AI-powered learning tools, and virtual labs that make every lesson engaging and effective.

GPS-Tracked Transport

Safe, comfortable, and GPS-monitored school buses with real-time tracking for parents, covering 40+ routes across the city.

Sports & Activities

Olympic-size swimming pool, cricket ground, basketball courts, football field, and 25+ extracurricular clubs to explore.

Expert Faculty

Highly qualified teachers with an average of 12+ years of experience, trained in modern pedagogy and student-centric learning.

Research Labs

State-of-the-art Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs equipped with the latest instruments to foster scientific curiosity.

Safe Campus

24/7 CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, and a fully walled campus ensuring complete child safety and security.

Global Exposure

Student exchange programs, Model UN conferences, and international competitions to broaden horizons and build global citizens.

Wellness Centre

Dedicated school counselors, a medical clinic, and mindfulness programs to ensure the mental and physical health of every student.
